📖 Legend & Detailed Explanation

Acronyms

  • CAC: Customer Acquisition Cost. Money spent to get one paying order (ads, influencer pay-out, QR print, creative fees). Excludes COGS & shipping.
  • CVR: Conversion Rate. Orders ÷ Website Sessions. 2.5 % = 25 orders per 1 000 visitors.
  • AOV: Average Order Value. Revenue ÷ Orders. Raise via bundles, upsells, free-shipping threshold.
  • ROAS: Return on Ad Spend. Revenue ÷ Ad Spend. 2.5 means ₹2.5 back for every ₹1 spent.
  • RTO: Return to Origin. COD orders cancelled before delivery / returned. Hurts cash & courier score.
  • LTV: Lifetime Value. Net profit you expect from a customer over their life. We use 60-day simplified model.
  • MOQ: Minimum Order Quantity. Smallest carton count a distributor must buy first time.
  • UGC: User-Generated Content. Videos, pics, reviews shot by real buyers; used in ads & reels.
  • P-Max: Google Performance Max. Automated campaign across Search, YouTube, Gmail, Discover.
  • CAPI: Conversions API. Sends purchase event from server to Meta, bypassing browser blocks.
  • QR: Quick-Response code sticker on retail shelf; links to WhatsApp or product page.

Formulas Used

  • Blended CAC: Σ(Channel-CAC × Channel-traffic-share)
  • Revenue: (Paid traffic + Organic traffic) × CVR × AOV + Distributors × OpeningOrder
  • Gross Profit: Revenue × Gross-Margin %
  • Net Margin: (Gross Profit – Total Ad Spend) ÷ Revenue
  • LTV (60d): AOV × (1 + Repeat-60d × 2 cycles)
  • LTV/CAC: LTV ÷ Blended CAC (target ≥3)

Detailed Summary for Stakeholders

Objective (90 days): Profitably sell 1,000 orders, build first-party data spine, onboard 15 distributors. We will achieve this by running a dual funnel: B2C (quiz-led, COD-friendly) and B2B (LinkedIn ABM + trade-show QR funnels). Paid media will be capped at 65 % of total spend; remaining 35 % comes from influencer rev-share, SEO blogs, and in-store QR codes. Creatives follow a 3-day refresh rule to combat ad fatigue and keep CAC ≤ ₹280. All tools start on free tiers; upgrade gates are revenue-linked (≥₹25 L/mo). Channel-level CAC sliders allow real-time re-allocation: if Meta CAC spikes, budget automatically tilts toward Google P-Max or influencer CPA deals. RTO is managed via WhatsApp COD confirmation bot and “pre-order” toggle during stock-outs. Net margin target ≥12 % by day 90, scaling to 20 % at ₹1 Cr monthly run-rate. Breakeven month 11 under base-case; downside protected by variable freelancer pool and zero long-term ad commitments.
